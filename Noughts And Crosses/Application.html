Tic Tac Toe

How To Play:

The purpose of this game is to get three in a row, column or diagonal.

To elaborate that, you are the "X". To start the game you will have to click anywhere in the grid, placing your "X". You will be playing against an AI player, and it will be completing against you to get three matching in a row, column or diagonal.

After competing with the AI player, you will get three different result. The result will differ and only one of three will be presented after the game finish.

The three result will be presented on the table, and it will be presented after you win, draw or lose against the AI player. If you were to win, the three cells in the table you connect will be change the color to green, drawing will change the all the cells within the table to blue and if you were to lose, the three matching cells from the AI player will change to red.

Below are illustrated images to give you a visual understanding of the game:

The cells changes color for a reason, this is due to the game corresponding with Phillip Hue Lighting. By winning, the light will change to green, drawing will change the light to blue and by losing, it will change it to red.
